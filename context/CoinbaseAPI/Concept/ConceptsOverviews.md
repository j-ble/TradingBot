# Coinbase Exchange Concepts Overview

Coinbase Exchange offers institutional-grade APIs tailored for spot trading, market making, and portfolio management. The platform supports Crypto-as-a-Service (CaaS) integration, institutional trading strategies, and high-volume market operations.
